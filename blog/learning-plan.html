Learning Plan

1.What is your long term goal or career pathway?

To be able to build websites and be invloved in a web development career

2.A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My key strengths are perseverance, adaptability, and curiosity while my main limitations are the ability to stay focus, procrasination and struggling to ask for assistance.

3.What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Be able to get distracted less and be more open to ask for assistance.

4.A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I like to have a skim thruough the sprint before I start, have a look the the timeboxes and divide the workload evenly across the week. When things are building up, I would try to get as much work done as possible at the start instead of dividing the workload, this way there will be extra time later in the week for work that went over the timebox limit.

5.A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will try to seek help whenever I encounter challenges beyond my ability to manage alone, by reaching out to facilitators, others in my cohort, and friends.

6.A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

To be there to help when needed during work hours (maybe after hours when things are starting to build up).
