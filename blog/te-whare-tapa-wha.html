Te Whare Tapa Whā

Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing) Give family members a call once a week
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Take breaks during study/work to reduce mental overload
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Reflect on personal values and what gives life meaning
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Take a walk after dinner daily
